## The Core Features That Actually Matter

### God Mode — Build a Hyper-Targeted Buyers List Instantly

This is the crown jewel. Type in any property address in America and God Mode shows you exactly who's been buying in that area — how much they're paying, what type of properties they target, how often they buy, and what their average hold time is.

Then you click "add to buyers list" and you're marketing to them before most wholesalers even know they exist.

It's not scraping random contacts from the internet. It's real transaction data on real active buyers. That difference is everything.

### Artemis Mode — Know Who's Interested Before They Submit an Offer

Once your deal goes live, Artemis Mode tracks every buyer who views it, how long they spent on the listing, whether they came back, and scores them based on buying intent.

So instead of blasting 500 people and hoping someone calls, you're calling the three buyers who looked at your deal four times this morning. That's a completely different conversation.

### InvestorLift Buyers Network — 5.5 Million Pre-Loaded, Vetted Buyers

Every plan comes with access to the platform's buyer network. Depending on your plan tier, you can market your deals directly to 1,000 up to 1 million+ pre-loaded, vetted investors across the country. The platform reportedly sells 3,000–5,000 properties per month — more than all U.S. hedge funds combined buy in a given month, times four (their claim, but the volume is real).

### AI Autopilot — Set It and Let It Run

Launched in 2025, AI Autopilot handles your marketing campaigns automatically. The AI identifies the most relevant buyers using transaction data and engagement history, then sends targeted email and SMS blasts on your behalf. You can run it fully on autopilot or configure campaigns manually — your call.
